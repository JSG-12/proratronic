Product Description

The PR-AL200 industrial audible alarm is designed to provide clear, unmistakable warning signals in even the noisiest environments. With its 120dB output, this alarm ensures that your safety messages are heard above machinery, equipment, and other industrial noise.

Applications

  • Factory and warehouse safety systems
  • Emergency evacuation alarms
  • Machine status and fault warnings
  • Process control and automation systems
  • Construction site safety

Installation

The PR-AL200 comes with a stainless steel mounting bracket for easy installation on walls, ceilings, or machinery. The unit features screw terminals for simple wiring and is compatible with most industrial control systems.

Technical Highlights

This alarm features selectable tone patterns including continuous, intermittent, and pulsed modes. The high-efficiency transducer provides maximum sound output with minimal power consumption, making it suitable for battery-backed systems.
