Product Description

The PR-SA400 forklift over speed alarm is a safety device that alerts operators when the vehicle exceeds preset speed limits. It typically uses sensors to monitor speed and triggers visual/audible warnings to prevent accidents. This system enhances workplace safety by reducing risks of collisions, tip-overs, or load spills caused by excessive speed. Modern alarms may integrate with fleet management systems to record speed violations. Proper speed control helps protect operators, pedestrians, and inventory while complying with safety regulations.

Applications

  • Factory and warehouse safety systems
  • Emergency evacuation alarms
  • Machine status and fault warnings
  • Process control and automation systems
  • Construction site safety

Installation

The PR-SA400 comes with screw hole for easy installation on forklift, or machinery. The unit features screw terminals for simple wiring and is compatible with most industrial control systems.
