About Proratronic

Engineering Safety for the Modern Workplace

Our Mission

At Proratronic, we believe that workplace safety shouldn't be compromised. Our mission is to develop innovative, reliable safety solutions that protect workers, prevent accidents, and create safer industrial environments worldwide.

Through continuous research and development, we strive to stay at the forefront of safety technology, anticipating industry needs before they arise.

Our Values

  • Safety First: Every product we design puts worker safety above all else

  • Innovation: We challenge conventional thinking to develop better solutions

  • Quality: Rigorous testing ensures our products perform when it matters most

  • Global Impact: We're committed to improving workplace safety worldwide

Our History

Company Founded

2010

Proratronic was established with a focus on industrial audible alarms, starting operations in a small facility with just 5 employees.

First Major Contract

2012

Secured our first large-scale contract with a major automotive manufacturer, leading to expansion of our production capabilities.

Product Line Expansion

2015

Launched our forklift safety series, marking our entry into vehicle safety systems and significantly broadening our market.

International Expansion

2018

Established distribution partnerships in Europe and North America, beginning our transition to a global safety solutions provider.

New Headquarters

2022

Moved into our state-of-the-art 50,000 sq ft facility, tripling our production capacity and R&D capabilities.
